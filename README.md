## 🛠 How to Use the App

Once installed, here are simple steps to get started:

- **Create a new project**  
  Open the app and select “New Project.” Name your project to reflect your donor or program.

- **Build a Theory of Change**  
  Use the tools provided to outline your objectives, outcomes, and activities. The software guides you through this process using clear prompts.

- **Set SMART indicators**  
  Add indicators that are Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. The app helps you define and organize these.

- **Align with SDGs**  
  Select relevant Sustainable Development Goals for your project. This ensures your work fits global development standards.

- **Develop monitoring plans**  
  Plan how and when to collect data for each indicator. The tool includes templates to make this easier.

- **Save and export your model**  
  Save your work regularly. You can export your logic model and reports as PDF or Excel files to share with donors or teams.
